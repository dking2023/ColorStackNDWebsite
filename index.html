What we do

Community, academics, and careers

Our events, initiatives, and programs are organized around three core pillars.

Pillar · Community

Find your people

General meetings and events where you can find your people. Our Buddy System also pairs new members with upperclass mentors from day one.

Pillar · Academics

Survive the curriculum

Connect with experienced students who've already been in your shoes for honest advice, homework support, study tips, and exam preparation throughout your CS journey.

Pillar · Careers

Launch your career

Resume reviews, mock interviews, recruiter visits, alumni talks from professionals in the industry, and access to the national ColorStack resume book that top companies recruit from each year.

Why join

Membership is free — and it follows you past graduation.

  • Free membership in the national ColorStack Family, 14,000+ students across 1000+ schools.
  • A local chapter that knows Notre Dame, our courses, our professors, and our recruiting timelines.
  • Board leadership opportunities become available each year for members looking to make a lasting impact.
